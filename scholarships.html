Steps to Apply for a Scholarship

  1. Explore available scholarship programs
  2. Check eligibility requirements
  3. Select your preferred language course

  5. Fill out the scholarship application form
  6. Submit your application online
  7. Application review by our admissions team
  8. Scholarship confirmation via email or phone
  9. Complete enrollment and start your journey
